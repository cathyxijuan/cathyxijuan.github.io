Welcome

UBC Structural Equation Modelling Lab (UBC SEM LAB) is a quantitative method lab located in the Department of Psychology at the University of British Columbia, and is directed by Dr. Victoria Savalei.

UBC SEM LAB conducts cutting-edge research in the area of SEM for psychological and behavioral science data. SEM is a powerful statistical modeling tool that allows researchers to test complex theories involving multiple observed and latent variables.
